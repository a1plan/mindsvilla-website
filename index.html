Our Story

We brought the world
to our village.

Mindsvillaa Academy was born in Chinnakuruli — a small village near Pandavapura in Mandya district, Karnataka. A place where children had never seen a robot, never written a line of code, and never imagined that AI was something they could learn.

Starting from a home, with a whiteboard, robotics kits, and an unshakeable belief in every child's potential, our founders built Mandya's first AI and Robotics home academy.
